Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2024

This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of personal information through our digital products and services, including Yug Vandana (Yug Vandana.com). You have the right to access, correct, and request the deletion of your personal data, with certain limitations on access to our products and services.

Data Retention

We retain personal data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services.

Security

We are committed to the security of your data, implementing encryption and regular security standards.

Children's Privacy

Yug Vandana is intended for use by individuals who have reached the legal age in their jurisdiction and reside in regions where such use is permissible. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under the legal age.

We may modify this policy at any time. Continued use of Yug Vandana signifies acceptance of these changes.

Contact Us

Agreement

By using Preline products and services, you acknowledge your understanding and agreement to this Privacy Policy.

Privacy Changes
